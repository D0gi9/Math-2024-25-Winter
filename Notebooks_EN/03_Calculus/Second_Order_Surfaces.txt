        "\n",
        "**No, they do not have any common points**\n",
        "\n",
        "**Reasoning**:\n",
        "\n",
        "The equation x² + y² + z² = 1 represents a sphere centered at the origin (0, 0, 0) with a radius of 1.\n",
        "\n",
        "\n",
        "The equation x² + y² + z² = 2 represents a sphere centered at the origin (0, 0, 0) with a radius of √2.\n",
        "\n",
        "\n",
        "Since the two spheres have the same center but different radii, they are concentric.\n",
        "\n",
        "\n",
        "Concentric spheres with different radii do not intersect, meaning they have no common points."

        "\n",
        "\n",
        "**to find the intersection, we need to solve the two equations**\n",
        "\n",
        "(x - 1)² + y² + z² - (x² + y² + z²) = 1 - 1\n",
        "\n",
        "This simplifies to:\n",
        "\n",
        "(x - 1)² - x² = 0\n",
        "\n",
        "Expanding and simplifying further, we get:\n",
        "\n",
        "x² - 2x + 1 - x² = 0\n",
        "\n",
        "-2x + 1 = 0\n",
        "\n",
        "x = 1/2\n",
        "\n",
        "**Substituting back**:\n",
        "\n",
        "Now that we have the value of x, we can substitute it back into either of the original equations to find the equation of the curve\n",
